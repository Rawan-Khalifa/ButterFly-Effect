Having known that trains and subways work almost all day, we decided to benefit from the concept of changing the mechanical energy into electrical energy. We made our solution to use the force of the carriages applied on the rails.


For doing so, we had to make an extension above the rails which contains springs; when those springs are compressed, they make the gear line connected to the rail to move the gear system which rotates the generator creating electricity.


We applied communication to our prototype by using voltage current sensor then connect the measured data with NodeMCU which transfer them to our website that characterizes technology.
